What Is ImpactLink?

ImpactLink is a digital platform that connects ambitious students with credible NGOs and nonprofits — enabling young people to gain real-world experience, build meaningful portfolios, and contribute to social good. For NGOs, it offers an easy, efficient way to find motivated volunteers and interns who are aligned with their mission.
