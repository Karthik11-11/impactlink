About ImpactLink

Creating a Seamless Zero-Cost Ecosystem

ImpactLink is a simple, smart, and meaningful platform that bridges the gap between students seeking real-world experience and NGOs looking for passionate contributors.

We make it easy for young people to discover internships, volunteer roles, and social impact projects that match their interests, skills, and availability. At the same time, NGOs gain access to motivated students who truly want to make a difference.
