What Is ImpactLink?

ImpactLink is a simple digital bridge that helps students find meaningful internships, volunteering roles, and social impact projects—while helping NGOs quickly access motivated talent with zero hassle.

For NGOs

Find Skilled Volunteers

Save Time

No complicated hiring process—just fast, direct connections.

Grow Your Mission

Expand your impact by bringing in motivated young contributors.
